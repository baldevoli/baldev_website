About Me

Hello! I'm Baldev Oli, a passionate Computer Science student at the University of Maryland Baltimore County (UMBC). With a strong foundation in programming and a keen interest in data science, I enjoy building innovative solutions and exploring the intersection of technology and real-world problems.

My journey in computer science began at the Community College of Baltimore County, where I earned my Associate's degree, and continues at UMBC as I pursue my Bachelor's degree. I'm particularly drawn to full-stack development, where I can bring ideas to life through clean, efficient code and intuitive user experiences.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or sharing my learning journey through my blog. I believe in continuous learning and staying up-to-date with the latest trends in technology.
